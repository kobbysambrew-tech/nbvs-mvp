NBVS Ghana

Building Ghana’s trusted national background verification system.

The National Background Verification System (NBVS) is a modern digital platform designed to streamline and secure background checks, criminal records, and driving history verification across Ghana. Our goal is to empower institutions, employers, and government agencies with fast, reliable, and transparent identity validation.
